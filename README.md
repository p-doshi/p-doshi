

# 🌊 Hey, I'm Parth 👋  
AI + ML researcher working on how vessels move, why they move that way, and how machines can learn it without hallucinating.


# 💫 About Me  
I work at the intersection of **machine learning**, **physics-based modeling**, and **maritime intelligence**.  

I enjoy building systems that don’t fall apart when no one’s watching — from generative trajectory models to AIS preprocessing pipelines to low-power edge deployments on Jetson devices.

If it involves **trajectory modeling**, **neural networks**, **data fusion**, or **making sense of chaotic marine signals**, I'm probably into it.

---

# 🚢 What I Work On  
- 🛳 **Vessel trajectory modeling**  
  Generative + physics-based approaches for realistic movement prediction.

- 🌍 **AIS data engineering**  
  Denoising, segmenting, interpolating — turning messy marine data into usable signals.

- ⚡ **Jetson Nano low-power AI systems**  
  Making models survive on tiny devices with even tinier power budgets.

- 🧠 **BCI / EEG signal decoding**  
  SSVEP classification with a focus on occipital activity and clean low-latency pipelines.
